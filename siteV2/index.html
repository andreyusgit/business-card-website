Who am i ?
I am a student of St. Petersburg Polytechnic University. In the first year I got acquainted with programming languages ​​for the first time and it was C #, I studied it as part of the curriculum, wrote the final project and abandoned it.

Already at the beginning of the second course, I began to study C ++ as part of the curriculum, the translator wrote in it. I liked programming, but I was burning for others - I really wanted to write my own telegram bot and I decided to succumb to that direction.

At the beginning of 2022, I wrote my first lines in Python and away we go. I wrote telegram bots one by one and continued to improve my skills in Python.

Moreover, in the spring of 2022 I start learn theory of testing in parallel. And already in the summer of 2022, I was ready to become a QA engineer and started looking for a job.

I went to a large number of interviews and got rejected for all of them, each time I filled in the gaps in my memory and upgraded my skills, and lo and behold, I succeeded! On July 13, I was hired by Ozon Tech as a QA automation engineer. All my efforts paid off.

Since then I have been working there, but my craving for new things has not disappeared, so I decided to create this website so that anyone can see my skills.
